# Databases for speed limit data and for speed camera data

Version:  March 2026

Below you will find the countries and their 2-character ISO code.
Europe is split up in its regions as Europe would otherwise be to big in one 7z.
Below in the assets you will find the grouped compressed sqlite databases.
* Road speed limits : \<region or continent\>_speedlimit.7z
 * Speed cameras: \<continent\>_camera.7z

**For info on how to use them, and what the benefits are,  read** [Local Databases](https://github.com/hvdwolf/SpeedAlert/blob/main/LocalDatabases.md).
